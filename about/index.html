About Us

Parents helping parents keep kids safe.

Our Mission

Small Ones Safety was created by parents and child safety professionals who believe every family deserves access to clear, practical safety guidance. We combine pediatric safety research, product testing, and real-world parenting experience to create resources that are actually useful — not just anxiety-inducing lists of everything that could go wrong, but actionable steps that make a real difference in keeping children safe while letting them be kids.
