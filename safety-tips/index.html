Safety Tips

Practical, age-specific guidance for every environment.

Babyproofing Essentials

Outlet covers, cabinet locks, gate installation, cord management, furniture anchoring, and safe sleep practices. A complete guide for preparing your home for a mobile baby.

Kitchen Safety

Hot surface guards, knife storage, cleaning product lockout, high chair safety, and age-appropriate cooking involvement. The kitchen is the most dangerous room — here's how to make it safe.

Water Safety

Bath supervision, pool fencing, flotation device selection, swim lesson timing, and open water awareness. Drowning is preventable with the right precautions.

Online & Digital Safety

Age-appropriate screen time, privacy settings, social media monitoring, cyberbullying recognition, and having productive conversations about online risks without creating fear.
